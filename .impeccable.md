## Design Context - Imbianchini Padova

### Users
Normal, regular customers (local homeowners and property owners in Padova and Padua province). They are looking for precise, clean, reliable, and professional solutions for house painting, exterior protective tinteggiatura, wood varnishing (shutters, balconies, beams), thermal active wall treatments, and modern decorative work.

### Brand Personality
**Modern, Clean, Precise, Trustworthy.** The brand needs to feel highly professional, approachable, and technically expert in painting technologies, anti-mold treatments, and elegant spatial colors.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Light, Organic, and Technical.** A bright and clean interface flooded with light, utilizing organic touches (e.g., soft natural colors like sage green and slate blue) combined with a technical, precise edge (structured grids, sharp typography). It must completely pivot away from heavy, dark interfaces, evoking the clean air, spaciousness, and refined architectural light of the Veneto plain.

### Design Principles
1. **Accessibility First**: High contrast ratios, large legible default typography, and clear visual hierarchy to ensure all demographics easily navigate the site.
2. **Speed & Clarity**: Progressive disclosure of information. Straightforward language, transparent pricing, and unambiguous calls to action.
3. **Organic Precision**: Blend technical reliability (clean lines, tight grids, state-of-the-art paint products) with organic warmth (light backgrounds, natural sage/slate color accents) to build trust.
4. **Reduced Cognitive Load**: Avoid excessive or distracting animations. Motion should only be used to guide focus or confirm actions.
